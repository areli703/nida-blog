# GoHighLevel vs SiteSwan: Which Platform Fits Your Agency Model?

GoHighLevel and SiteSwan are often mentioned in the same breath. Both white-label. Both agency-friendly. Both promise recurring revenue.

But they're not the same thing. They serve different business models, different skill levels, and different end goals. Here's the honest breakdown.

---

## What Each Platform Actually Is

**GoHighLevel** is an all-in-one marketing automation platform. CRM, funnels, SMS, email, booking, reputation, social — a toolbox.

**SiteSwan** is a white-label website builder with light CRM and local marketing tools baked in. A website + SEO machine with extras.

The core DNA is different. GHL is automation-first. SiteSwan is web presence-first.

---

## Where SiteSwan Wins

### 1. Faster Web Builds
SiteSwan is built for churning out local business websites. Their templates are industry-specific (plumbers, dentists, restaurants). You can launch a clean site in an afternoon.

GHL can build pages, but it's not a website builder at heart. It takes longer and looks less polished without custom work.

### 2. Lower Monthly Cost
At $199–$299/mo, SiteSwan costs less than GHL's realistic $297+ plan. Fewer surprise add-ons, too.

### 3. SEO Tools Out of the Box
SiteSwan includes local SEO features — directory submissions, GBP integration, keyword tracking — without extra configuration.

GHL requires you to build or buy SEO workflows separately.

### 4. Less Client Confusion
SiteSwan's client dashboards are simpler. Fewer buttons. Fewer ways for a local business owner to break something accidentally.

---

## Where GoHighLevel Wins

### 1. Marketing Automation Depth
If you need multi-step SMS/email sequences, behavior-based triggers, If/Else logic, and advanced pipelines, GHL is miles ahead.

### 2. True SaaS Resale Model
GHL's SaaS mode lets you resell the platform itself as your own software, earning $200–$500 per seat in passive income. SiteSwan doesn't support this model as cleanly.

### 3. Course/Membership Hosting
GHL has native course hosting. SiteSwan does not. If your offer includes training or memberships, GHL wins.

### 4. Community & Marketplace
GHL's ecosystem (templates, snapshots, courses, Facebook groups) is massive. SiteSwan's is smaller.
