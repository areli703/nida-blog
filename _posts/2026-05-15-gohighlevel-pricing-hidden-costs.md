# GoHighLevel Pricing: The Hidden Costs Nobody Talks About

The GoHighLevel pricing page says "$97/month." That's a starting point. The real number is much higher — and most "GHL agencies" don't realize it until they're already committed.

Here's the real cost breakdown for a solo operator running a small agency with 3–5 clients.

---

## 1. Platform Fee

| Plan | Monthly Cost | What You Actually Need |
|---|---|---|
| Starter ($97) | $97/mo | Too limited: 1 sub-account, no white-label |
| Unlimited ($297) | $297/mo | White-label + unlimited accounts. The realistic minimum. |
| SaaS Pro ($497) | $497/mo | Required if reselling GHL as software to many clients. |

**Realistic platform cost:** $297/month

---

## 2. Required Add-Ons

GHL nickel-and-dimes you on essentials:

| Add-On | Typical Monthly Cost |
|---|---|
| Additional phone numbers | $1.20–$3/number |
| SMS credits (beyond basics) | $10–$50+ |
| Email sending overages | $10–$30 |
| Premium snapshots/templates | $47–$297 one-time or monthly |
| Reputation management widget | Bundled, but usage-limited |
| Custom domains | Free, but SSL/config time costs |

**Typical add-on total:** $50–$150/month

---

## 3. Integrations You End Up Buying

GHL claims "all-in-one," but gaps exist:

| Tool | Why You Need It | Monthly Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Zapier | Connect apps GHL doesn't natively support | $19.99–$49 |
| Canva / Design tool | Funnel graphics, social posts | $12.99–$14.99 |
| Loom / Video hosting | Sales videos, walkthroughs | $12.99–$15 |
| Stripe transaction fees | Just reality | 2.9% + $0.30/transaction |
| Calendly (backup) | If GHL booking breaks | $10–$12 |

**Typical integration total:** $50–$100/month

---

## 4. The Time Cost (This Is the Killer)

This is the hidden cost no pricing calculator shows:

| Task | Estimated Hours | Value at $50/hr (your time) |
|---|---|---|
| Initial GHL setup & configuration | 20–40 hrs | $1,000–$2,000 |
| Troubleshooting automations | 5–10 hrs/mo | $250–$500/mo |
| Keeping up with GHL updates | 2–4 hrs/mo | $100–$200/mo |
| Training/supporting confused clients | 3–6 hrs/mo | $150–$300/mo |
| Rebuilding broken workflows | 2–5 hrs/mo | $100–$250/mo |

**Time cost burden:** $600–$1,250/month in *your* labor
