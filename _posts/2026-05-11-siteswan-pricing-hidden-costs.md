SiteSwan advertises a flat monthly fee. But running a reseller agency costs way more than just the platform subscription.

Here's every dollar you'll actually spend.

## SiteSwan Platform Fee

SiteSwan uses a tiered monthly licensing model. Plans range from approximately $150-$300/month depending on features and included client sites.

**What you get**:
- White-label website builder
- 100+ industry templates
- Client sites (5-50 depending on plan)
- Training materials
- Email support

## Hidden Cost #1: Tools SiteSwan Doesn't Include

SiteSwan is a website builder. Running an agency requires more.

| Missing Tool | Typical Alternative | Monthly Cost |
|---|---|---|
| CRM | GoHighLevel, HubSpot | $97-$297 |
| Email marketing | Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign | $29-$149 |
| Booking/scheduling | Calendly, Acuity | $10-$20 |
| Live chat | Intercom, Tidio | $39-$99 |
| Automation | Zapier, Make | $19-$99 |
| Analytics | Google Analytics + Data Studio | $0-$49 |
| Invoicing | QuickBooks, FreshBooks | $15-$50 |

**Total for a complete agency stack**: $200-$800/month in addition to SiteSwan.

## Hidden Cost #3: Your Time

Building a client site takes 3-8 hours. But non-billable work takes just as long:

- Prospecting: 2-5 hrs per client
- Sales calls/proposals: 1-3 hrs
- Content gathering: 2-4 hrs
- Revisions: 1-3 hrs
- Training/handoff: 0.5-1 hrs
- Support: 1-2 hrs/month

**Total: 8-18 hours upfront + 1-2 hours/month**

If you value your time at $50/hour, that's $400-$900 in real cost per client.

## The Core Problem

SiteSwan's flat fee is honest. But the platform doesn't include CRM, automation, chatbots, or lead generation tools. When you add those separately, the "complete agency stack" costs more than an all-in-one platform.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is SiteSwan worth the cost?

Yes, if you only sell websites and do your own active client acquisition. If you want monthly systems, the economics favor platforms with broader features.

### What's the minimum viable budget?

$300/month total realistic minimum. You need 1 client every 2 months to break even.

### Which is cheaper overall?

SiteSwan is cheapest for pure website reselling. Nida is cheapest for agencies selling complete monthly systems.
